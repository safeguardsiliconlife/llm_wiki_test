# ForbesPredict

Prediction market platform embedded in Forbes news articles, launched January 2026 in partnership with [[Axiom]]. Uses a token economy (no real money) to gamify news engagement. Positioned as Forbes' response to [[AI Media Traffic Disruption]] eating its referral traffic.

## Mechanics
- Token-based; tokens have no cash value
- New users: 800 tokens on arrival; 800/day login bonus
- Predicting an outcome costs 100 tokens; "hints" (crowd probability preview) cost 10
- Tokens unlock status and gameplay advantages within the Forbes ecosystem

## Stated Rationale
Forbes CIO Nina Gould: "AI is fundamentally changing how people access information... Our response isn't to chase scale, but to deepen engagement."

## Criticism
- Embedded on a story about 8 children killed in Shreveport, Louisiana (2026-04-19), asking readers to predict gun control legislation — see [[Forbes Prediction Market Gamifies Mass Shooting Story]]
- Described as an "ersatz Polymarket" that combines watered-down aggregation journalism with stakes-free gambling
- Exemplar of the [[Depravity Economy]]
