# Figma

Browser-based collaborative design tool founded mid-2010s. Pioneered running a complex design application fully in-browser using WebGL and asm.js. Overtook Sketch as the dominant UI/UX design tool; cross-platform by nature (Sketch is Mac-only).

## Key Facts
- ~2,000 employees (as of 2026)
- Userbase (Q1 2025 S1): 33% designers, 30% developers, 37% other non-design roles
- Attempted acquisition by Adobe was blocked on competition grounds
- AI product: **Figma Make** — considered underwhelming; uses Sonnet 4.5 (sometimes Gemini) for inference

## AI Vulnerability

The majority of Figma's userbase consists of non-designers (PMs, executives, developers) who adopted it for collaboration. These are exactly the roles AI agents can substitute. See [[Figma's Woes Compound with Claude Design]] for analysis.

Figma pays Anthropic for inference to run Figma Make, effectively funding [[Claude Design]] — a more capable direct competitor running on Opus 4.7.

## TAM Expansion Products
- **Dev Mode** — for developers (now undercut by LLMs)
- **Slides** — presentation tool competing with PowerPoint
- **Sites** — Webflow-like site builder
